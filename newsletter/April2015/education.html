How to Register & Top 10 Reasons to Attend AASA Midyear Retreats

To Register and More Information

  • Attendance is FREE for AASA Members and $50 for non-AASA Members.
  • Dallas: To register, please email Rebecca Napier.
  • To learn more, visit http://aasa1.org/dallas-midyear-retreat.
  • Seattle: To register, please email Kira Martin.
  • To learn more, visit http://aasa1.org/seattle-midyear-retreat.

Top 10 Reasons to Attend an AASA Midyear Retreat

#10 Opportunity to explore a new city and enjoy a fun social event the evening prior.

#9 Fun and easy way to meet colleagues in similar roles and with similar challenges to your own.

#8 Work-related "break" from the current chaos of your day job.

#7 Mini-vacation if it is possible to stay over the weekend or even longer.

#6 Great content and relevant, engaging speakers.

#5 Ability to present or participate in a panel in a more informal, supportive setting.

#4 Reconnect with friends and colleagues from around the country; make new ones.

#3 Exposure to other academic medical center campuses, health system models and facilities.

#2 Opportunity for open dialogue and spontaneous discussion.

#1 Feeling connected to and supported by an organization committed to the professional growth of academic surgical leaders.
