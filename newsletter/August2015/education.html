AASA Annual Conference: Chicago, October 4-6

The Education Committee is delighted to provide AASA members with an update on the upcoming conference.

Earlier this year, the Education Committee sent out a survey to solicit topics for the upcoming conference. A BIG thank you to those of you who took a few minutes to provide some ideas and suggestions! The Committee has developed an education plan incorporating those suggestions into three core areas: the Annual Conference, our Webinar series and – of course – Cutting Edge articles.

One suggestion that resonated was to incorporate more opportunities to network with speakers throughout the conference. We hear you and have incorporated your feedback into planning this year’s agenda. There never seems to be enough time to talk one on one with the speakers after an engaging session.

Hotel Information

Our ACS partners have notified us that our conference hotel is Hilton Chicago at 720 S. Michigan Avenue. Room rates for AASA are $254 for the first 105 to register, and then increases to $284. The link to hotel registration is: https://www.tphousing.com/ph2/startres.aspx?EICode=3116&Attcode=1276

 

Conference Registration

There will be an early bird conference registration rate, same as last year, which is $500 for AASA members. Look for notices or check http://www.aasa1.org/education-events for more information.

In addition, the Committee would like to encourage conference attendees to make flight arrangements to Chicago early. As we know, any city that is hosting the ACS Clinical Congress becomes exceptionally busy so you will not want to wait until the last minute to book your flight.

If you have questions or would like to become involved with the conference planning, please contact Susan Marx or Teri Keeler for more information.

 

2015 AASA Poster Presentation Submissions

2015 marks the 3rd Annual Call for Poster Presentations to be showcased at our National Conference in Chicago. The “Windy City” is bringing about changes to the poster presentations so look for the AASA Email Blast in the coming weeks to see what is brewing.

  • Call for Poster Abstracts to be received by September 1, 2015
  • Judging will take place prior to the conference
  • All posters will be showcased during lunch on Monday
  • Top three posters will be selected and given dedicated time for presention on Tuesday
