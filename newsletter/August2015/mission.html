Gretchen Schwarze, MD

Informed Consent and Fewer Unwanted Surgeries

Vascular Specialist, the official newspaper of the Society for Vascular Surgery, reported on a study by Gretchen Schwarze, MD, that takes a modern approach to informed consent and may lead to fewer unwanted surgeries.

A vascular surgeon and clinical ethicist at the University of Wisconsin, Dr. Schwarze has received funding from NIH, National Institute on Aging GEMS-STAR and a supplemental Jahnigen Award for her two-year study "A communication tool to assist older adults facing difficult surgical decisions." You can read the full article online.
